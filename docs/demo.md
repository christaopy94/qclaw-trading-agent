## 1. Agent Orchestration Flow

The core of QClaw FinAgent: 5 specialized agents orchestrated through a structured pipeline.

![Agent Workflow Diagram](../screenshots/agent_workflow.png)

**Workflow Sequence:**
1. **Data Agent** collects raw market data
2. **Sentiment Agent** analyzes market emotion
3. **Strategy Agent** identifies themes and leaders
4. **Review Agent** generates structured report
5. **Notify Agent** delivers via webhook
